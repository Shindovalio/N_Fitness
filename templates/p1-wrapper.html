    What Is Percussion Therapy

    Percussive therapy (what massage guns do) provides rapid bursts of pressure into the body’s muscle tissue as the head of the gun moves back and forth.

    This repeated pounding of the muscles leads to increased blood flow, pain relief and range of motion to the muscle that is being treated.

    The NGun Effect
    Improving Blood Circulation
